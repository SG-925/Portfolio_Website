About Me

I'm a high school student who loves building things — whether that's PCB designs and robots for FRC competitions, financial analysis projects for FBLA, or 3D-printed designs. I'm always working on something.

Background

I'm based in Atlanta, Georgia, and fell in love with technology and design after building my first project. Since then, I've been passionate about PCB design, robotics, and 3D printing — combining hardware design with practical applications.

I'm a design and strategy team member in FRC robotics team #1683, competing in FBLA, a member of the AI club where I volunteer as a tutor, and a Life Scout. I love solving real problems through thoughtful engineering and design.

I'm focused on developing expertise in PCB design, CAD, and 3D printing while also building my skills in financial analysis and strategic thinking. I aim to combine these skills in my future career.
